Drip Coffee

House Blend Smooth, balanced, and always fresh — the perfect everyday cup.
Mansooned Malabar Bold and earthy with a rich finish — for coffee lovers who want something deeper.
Flavored Hot Coffees Comforting favorites with rotating flavors like Chocolate Macadamia, Apple Cinnamon, and more.

Espresso & Specialty

Espresso Duo / Macchiato Bold espresso with rich crema — classic café energy.
Cortados Perfectly balanced espresso + milk (with fun variations like Cuban, NOLA, Pistachio Milk Baklava, and more).
Cappuccino / Americano / Latte Timeless espresso staples — frothy, smooth, and always satisfying.
Chaiwala Latte / Dirty Chai Latte Spiced chai brewed with care — go “dirty” for an espresso-powered kick.
Matcha / Blue Matcha Butterfly Vibrant, creamy tea lattes — earthy, refreshing, and smooth.
Golden Milk Latte / Joe’s Hot Chocolate Warm, cozy, and comforting — caffeine-free options everyone loves.

Draft Brew

Cold Brew & Nitro Options Slow-steeped and ultra smooth — nitro adds a creamy, cascading texture.
Oat Milk Dirty Brew Creamy oat milk meets cold brew for a smooth, mellow sip.

Refreshers & Lemonades

Tea & Herbal Lemonades Bright, citrusy, and refreshing — perfect for warmer days.
